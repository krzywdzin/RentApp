## Integration Check

Two critical integration gaps were identified and fixed during audit:

1. **Mobile JWT Guard (FIXED):** `JwtAuthGuard` only tried `'jwt'` strategy, rejecting mobile tokens. Fixed to `AuthGuard(['jwt', 'jwt-mobile'])`.

2. **Web Token Refresh (FIXED):** `access_token` cookie maxAge was 30min, causing cookie deletion before refresh. Extended to 24h (JWT expiry enforced server-side, not cookie).

Both fixes committed: `20b28e8`.
